Emergency Care

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please call 911 immediately. We offer 24/7 emergency care services for life-threatening conditions, including heart attacks, strokes, and severe injuries. The Emergency Medical Services (EMS) promptly dispatches well equipped ambulances staffed by well trained paramedics. The staff are certified on regular basis following up-to-date evidence-based protocols.

A specialist doctor is also involved in pre-hospital care in transferring critically ill patients. The goal of EMS is not only to safely transfer patients to the definite care at the nearest hospital as quickly as possible,but also to assess patients and commence essential and potentially life-saving treatment at the scene and during transfer.

Cardiology

Our cardiology department provides comprehensive care for patients with heart and vascular conditions, including:

  • Coronary artery disease
  • Heart failure
  • Arrhythmias
  • Valvular heart disease

Pediatrics

We offer specialized care for children, including:

  • General pediatric care
  • Childhood immunizations
  • Pediatric surgery
  • Neonatal intensive care

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic department provides advanced care for a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions, including:

  • Fractures and dislocations
  • Arthritis
  • Joint replacement surgery
  • Sports injuries

Neurology

Our neurology department offers comprehensive care for patients with neurological disorders, including:

  • Stroke
  • Epilepsy
  • Multiple sclerosis
  • Alzheimer's disease
